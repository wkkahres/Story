The Mail"

"It was a dark and stormy night. The Dover mail was due at the usual hour; but it was late, and the driver had not yet come in. The weather was so cold and damp that the air was full of mist, and the ground was as slippery as ice. As the coach rumbled through the streets, the horses' hoofs seemed to echo louder and louder in the silence, and the sound of the wheels creaking in the mud was the only noise to be heard
